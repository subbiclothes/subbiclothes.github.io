Outfit Config Editor (OCE) is a free companion web tool for Subbi Clothes.
Configure each outfit visually — no notecard or JSON editing by hand.
Runs entirely in your browser, nothing to install.
Exports a ready-to-paste notecard straight into your HUD.
Imports existing config notecards, so nothing is lost when switching from manual to visual editing.
